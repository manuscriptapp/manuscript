Key Features

Project-Based Writing

Organize chapters, notes, and research in one place. Keep everything together like Scrivener, but open-source.

Markdown Support

Flexible formatting with portable files. Your writing stays readable and accessible forever.

Snapshots & History

Safe writing environment with undo history and snapshots. Never lose your work.

Cross-Platform

Native apps for iOS and macOS. Write anywhere on your Apple devices.

Free CloudKit Sync

Keep your projects synchronized across all your devices. No subscription required.

Open File Format

.manuscript files are portable, version-control friendly, and designed to be extensible.

Our Philosophy

Files First

Your writing belongs to you, not the cloud. Full control over your data.

Open Source

All code is visible and modifiable. No secrets, no lock-in.

Privacy First

CloudKit sync is optional and secure. No accounts required.

Community Driven

Feedback and contributions shape the project's future.

AI-Assisted Writing

Optional AI features to help with suggestions, rephrasing, and inspiration. You choose how and when to use them.

Free & Private

Apple Foundation Models

On-device AI that's free, fast, and completely private. Your writing never leaves your device.

Flexible

Bring Your Own AI

Use your own API keys to integrate OpenAI or other models. Full control over your AI setup.
