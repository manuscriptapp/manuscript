Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Manuscript is built to prioritize privacy and file ownership. This policy explains what data the app processes and how that data is handled.

Data we process

  • Your writing files: Stored locally in files you create and control.
  • Optional iCloud sync: If enabled by your Apple account, your project files sync through iCloud.
  • Optional API keys: If you use AI or speech integrations, API keys are stored on-device in secure storage.
  • Optional location data: Used only when a location-related feature is used by you.

What we do not do

  • We do not require account registration for Manuscript.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not run advertising trackers in the app.

Third-party services

Any third-party services are optional and user-initiated (for example, AI providers when configured by the user). Their use is governed by each provider's own privacy policy.

Your choices

  • Delete projects at any time by removing files from your device or iCloud Drive.
  • Export your work at any time in supported formats.
  • Remove optional API keys from app settings.

Contact

Questions or requests: manuscriptapp@proton.me.
